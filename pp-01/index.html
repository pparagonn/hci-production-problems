Playfair Display and Source Sans Pro Complemented

Reasoning behind typeface selection:

Playfair Display has a transitional design from when broad nib quills were replaced by pointed steel pens. Since Playfair Display has print letterforms of high contrast and delicate hairlines, it pairs well with the more simplistic Source Sans Pro. In regard to strategies, this would fall under "Embrace the Other" because of their duplexity in differences.

According to medium.com :

"As the name indicates, Playfair Display is well suited for titling and headlines."
